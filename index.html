FAQ

What is this coin?

Miner Cat is a meme coin inspired by a hardworking, adventurous cat exploring the world of crypto mining. 

What makes this coin special?

It combines the thrill of crypto mining with a quirky, determined mascot that resonates with enthusiasts. 

Why should I join the community?

Joining Miner Cat means becoming part of a community driven by shared goals, exclusive mining-themed rewards, and events. 

How can I purchase this coin?

Step 1
Install Phantom Wallet and add SOL
Step 2
Connect Phantom to Solana DEX (like Radium or Serum)
Step 3
Swap SOL for Miner Cat Coin
Step 4
Adjust Slippage and Fees
Step 5
Import Miner Cat Coin to Phantom Wallet

What is the coin’s vision?

The vision of Miner Cat is to foster a community-focused, mining-themed ecosystem that empowers crypto lovers. 

How can I stay updated about the project?

Follow Miner Cat on Twitter, Discord, and Instagram to stay in the loop about mining events and updates.
